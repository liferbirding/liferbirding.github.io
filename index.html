How it works

Three taps from curious to hooked.

No field guide to memorise. No quiz to pass. You watch, you log, you climb — and you learn the birds without ever feeling like you're studying.

01
Capture

Spot it. Get close. Take the shot.

Line up your camera and Lifer identifies what you've found. The clearer the photo, the higher the confidence — so creep a little closer and frame it well.

  • Identifies the bird in front of you, with a confidence read.
  • Works on the trail — captures queue and sync when you're back online.
02
Collect

Build an aviary that's only yours.

Every capture lands in your Aviary — your personal collection. Watch it grow from your first Magpie to the rare find you chased for weeks.

  • A real life list, open-ended.
  • Personalise your aviary. Tap any card for field notes.
03
Compete

Let the birding games begin.

Friends, leaderboards, streaks, multipliers, badges and more. Climb the global board, or just your flock's — friends you actually go birding with.

  • Global and friends leaderboards, your rank always in view.
  • Chase the feature species for extra points.
