Delete your account

Your captures are yours — including the right to remove them entirely. Here's exactly how, and exactly what happens when you do.

Processed within 30 days

01Two ways to delete

Deleting is permanent and removes your aviary, sightings, photos, friends and leaderboard standing.

Fastest · in the app

Delete in Lifer

  1. Open Lifer and tap your Profile.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion. That's it.
No longer have the app?

Delete by email

Send a request from the email address on your account to privacy@liferbirding.com, or use the form below. We'll verify it's you, delete your account, and confirm when it's done — within 30 days.

02What happens to your data

Removed

  • Your profile, display name and email
  • Every capture, photo and sighting
  • Your points, lifers, streak and rank
  • Your friend connections

Kept (cannot identify you)

  • Anonymised capture records — species, date and location, no longer linked to you — kept so community statistics and species data stay accurate
  • Records we're legally required to retain, for as long as the law requires

Deletion from our active systems is processed within 30 days. If you'd also like your anonymised capture records erased, just say so in your request — you can ask for full erasure at any time, as described in our Privacy Policy.

03Request deletion by email

Don't have the app handy? Fill this in and it'll open an email to our privacy team. We only act on requests from the account's own email address.

04Changed your mind?

No need to delete anything to take a break — just close the app. Your aviary waits, and your birds will still be out there when you come back. If you only want to stop notifications, you can turn those off in Settings → Notifications. Questions? privacy@liferbirding.com.
